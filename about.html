About

I'm Katie Rasch, a creative turned web developer who's passionate about building user-friendly, thoughtfully designed websites and applications. With a background in visual design and a love for clean, functional code, I bring a unique perspective to the development process: one that's equal parts aesthetic and strategic.

I'm experienced in front-end and back-end development, with a growing portfolio of full-stack applications built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, and more. I'm especially drawn to clean UI, meaningful user interaction, and the challenge of solving problems through code.

When I'm not coding, you can usually find me playing cozy video games and working on personal passion projects (often involving cats, drawing, or both).

I'm currently open to remote full-time or freelance opportunities and excited to collaborate on projects that are creative, purposeful, and user-focused.
